## About Insighta

Insighta is a platform designed to empower small-scale local businesses by transforming their existing data files into actionable insights.

It allows business owners to upload CSV or Excel files and instantly analyze their data without requiring a Business Analyst or technical expertise. By automating data understanding, query generation, and insight delivery, Insighta helps users make informed decisions quickly and confidently.
